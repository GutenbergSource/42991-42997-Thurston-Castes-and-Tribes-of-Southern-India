Volume six of a seven-volume set on the tribes and castes of Southern India. Not as difficult as the volumes on the Central Provinces (of which one volume is still pending), but still including a lot of names with macrons. This very nice set from 1909 has been standing in my bookshelf for 7 years, waiting for a way of scanning it without damaging the books. (And the last 3 volumes I don't even have to scan at all, as the Internet Archive now includes them!)
